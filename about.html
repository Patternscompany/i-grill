Invisible Grills Materials

Invisible grills are a modern, sleek, and safe solution for balconies, windows, and open spaces. Here's a breakdown of the materials used:

1. Stainless Steel Cables

Material: High-quality stainless steel, typically SS316 or SS316L.

Features:

  • Resistant to rust and corrosion.
  • Durable and strong, capable of withstanding significant weight.
  • Aesthetic and low maintenance.

Thickness: Usually ranges are 2mm, 2.5mm, 3mm.

Note: We recommend taking 2mm for best results.

2. Nylon Coating

Material: Clear nylon or PVC coating over stainless steel cables.

Purpose:

  • Provides additional protection against rust and wear.
  • Ensures smoothness to prevent injuries.
  • Protects against UV rays and moisture.

3. Aluminum Frames

Material: Lightweight but sturdy aluminum frames.

Features:

  • Rust-resistant and durable.
  • Available in various finishes (e.g., anodized or powder-coated).
  • Provides structural stability.

4. Accessories and Fittings

Materials: Stainless steel or aluminum brackets, screws, and tensioners.

Purpose:

  • Ensure proper alignment and maintain cable tension.
  • Offer long-lasting stability and safety.

Key Advantages

  • Transparency: Blends seamlessly with the surroundings.

  • Safety: Prevents falls, especially for children and pets.

  • Durability: Resistant to weathering, rust, and corrosion.

  • Low Maintenance: Requires minimal cleaning and upkeep.
