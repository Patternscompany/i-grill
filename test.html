Invisible Grills Installation Guide

Step-by-step process for a safe and precise installation.

1. Site Inspection and Measurement

Inspect the location and take accurate measurements to ensure the grills fit perfectly.

2. Material Preparation

Choose high-tensile stainless steel cables and prepare necessary hardware like screws, brackets, and anchors.

3. Frame Installation

Mark points, drill holes, and secure frames or mounting brackets to the structure.

4. Cable Installation

Fix the cables in a uniform pattern, using tensioners to ensure proper alignment.

5. Safety Testing

Check the stability and alignment of the cables to ensure durability and functionality.

6. Finishing Touches

Trim excess material, cover screws for a neat appearance, and polish the installation.

7. Maintenance

Inspect for damage, replace worn components, and re-tension cables to maintain functionality.
